About Jaylee Sorenson

Hi, I'm Jaylee Sorenson -- a writer passionate about storytelling, character journeys, and the small moments that make life feel real.
While I explore many form of writing, including poetry and nonfiction, my heart belons to fiction and novels. I've been writing for as long as I can remember, driven by a love of words and the way stories can connect us.
My work has been featured in The Southern Quill and on Route 7 Review, and I continue to grow and refine my craft as I move toward my dream of becoming a published novelist. Whether I'm writing a sweeping adventure, a quiet reflection, or a sharp review, my goal is always the same: to create something that resonates with leaders.
When I'm not lost in a draft or dreaming up new stories, you can find me on sharing glimpses of my writing journey on my instagram or outside participating in one of my many hobbies.

Thank you for being part of this adventure with me.

A few fun facts about me:

I wrote my first trilogy in 8th grade and have been coming up with stories ever since.
